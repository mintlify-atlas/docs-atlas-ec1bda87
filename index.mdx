The Polymarket Copy Trading Bot is an open-source Node.js bot that trades on Polymarket's prediction markets. It ships with two independent strategies: a BTC Up/Down 5-minute arbitrage engine that exploits complementary-token price gaps, and a dedicated copy-trading mode that mirrors the buy orders of any target wallet you choose to follow.

## Two trading modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BTC Up/Down arb" icon="arrows-left-right" href="/modes/arb-bot">
    Posts a dual-sided limit ladder on every 5-minute BTC market and takes aggressive fills when the combined ask falls below the arb threshold.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wallet copy trading" icon="user-group" href="/modes/copy-trader">
    Watches one or more target wallets and instantly mirrors their buy orders with FIXED, MIRROR, or RATIO sizing — all with configurable spend caps.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
